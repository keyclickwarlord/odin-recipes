Avocado Eggs

Yummy Avocado and Eggs!

I love this smashed avocado toast with egg! Thanks to a hard-boiled egg, flaky salt, lemon, and pepper, this avocado toast is healthy, protein-packed, delicious, and has significant sticking power! Jump to the Avocado Toast with Egg Recipe or read on to see our tips (including how to choose avocados).

Avocado toast works brilliantly for breakfast, lunch, and even for dinner. The star of this toast is the avocado, so it’s essential you use ripe avocados. Nobody likes slicing into an avocado and finding it’s underripe or worse, brown!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Toast
  • Avocado
  • Chile Powder
  • Chia Seeds
